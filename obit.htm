Courtney Gloria Bell – Kentville / Lower River Inhabitants

BELL Courtney Gloria, 23 of Kentville passed away September 14, 2018. Born in Halifax, she was a daughter of Gloria (Hayes) and the late James Steven Bell.

She will be deeply missed and forever loved by her common law husband Shawn Hickey and their son Caleb of Kentville; her mother Gloria and Stepfather John MacDonnell, Lower River Inhabitants; sister Deidre, Halifax; brothers Joseph Bell, Halifax; Lewis MacDonnell, Lower River Inhabitants; Maternal grandmother Gloria Hayes and step grandmother Isabell MacDonnell. Courtney will be missed by her many Aunts, Uncles and Cousins.

She was predeceased by her father James Steven Bell, maternal grandfather, Roderick James Hayes , paternal grandparents Bernard and Alice Bell and Step grandfather Lewis McKenna.

Resting In Green’s Funeral Home, Port Hawkesbury with visitation Wednesday from 6-9 pm. Funeral Mass will be offered Thursday at 11 a.m. from St. Francis de Sales Roman Catholic Church, burial to follow in the Parish Cemetery.

Memorial donations may be made to mental health unit of St. Martha’s Regional Hospital or to Fundy Interchurch food bank, Kentville.
Funeral arrangements are under the direction of Green’s Funeral Home, Port Hawkesbury.

Messages to the family may be conveyed at www.greensfuneralhome.ca
